Measurement & Verification (M&V)

In essence, Measurement and Verification (M&V) is the process of quantifying the energy savings achieved through different Energy Conservation Measures (ECMs). Without advanced technology, this process can be both complex and time-consuming. That's where Joule M&V comes in, simplifying and streamlining the entire process.

Joule M&V is your ultimate solution for real-time performance monitoring of your building. It empowers you to visualize data through dynamic charts and dashboards, whether you use our pre-defined KPIs and graphs or tailor your own. This comprehensive system gives you unparalleled insights into your energy efficiency.

When our JouleBox integrates with your Building Management System (BMS), it captures a continuous stream of sensor data every minute. This data is fed into our advanced Joule AnalytiX engines. Without effective filtering and organization, this influx of data can become overwhelming. Joule M&V expertly manages this challenge by transforming vast amounts of raw data into clear, actionable Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). These KPIs can be easily downloaded in various formats, such as CSV, allowing you to make strategic, data-driven decisions with confidence and ease.

Joule M&V transforms the way you monitor and optimize your energy conservation measures by simplifying and expediting the process in real-time. It provides an exceptional ability to track and analyze energy performance continuously, turning a vast amount of data into actionable insights. With interactive and customizable charts, you gain clear visibility into Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), enabling effective budgeting, immediate alerts, and strategic greenhouse gas (GHG) reduction planning.

Joule M&V doesn’t just monitor; it empowers you to make informed decisions and seize energy-saving opportunities with precision. Continuous measurement and verification are seamlessly provided through 24/7 remote monitoring, ensuring that you’re always ahead in the pursuit of energy efficiency. Utility Bill Management

Our comprehensive utility bill management module is designed to transform how organizations handle and optimize their utility expenses. With powerful features tailored to maximize efficiency and cost savings, it ensures that your utility bills are managed with precision and ease. Key features include:

Data collection and tracking:

Joule UAX streamlines your utility management by automatically collecting and monitoring data on electricity, natural gas, and water usage from utility bills and various other sources. This ensures you have real-time insights into your consumption, helping you optimize expenses with unparalleled efficiency.

Budgeting and forecasting:

Joule transforms your data into actionable insights by presenting it in intuitive graphs and charts. It generates comprehensive reports on usage patterns, costs, and potential savings opportunities, empowering you to make informed decisions and uncover significant savings with ease.

Integrating with other systems:

Joule takes the hassle out of managing utility bills by seamlessly handling invoice generation and payment processing. It ensures accurate tracking and efficient management of all your utility expenses, streamlining your financial operations and saving you valuable time.

Data visualization and reporting:

Joule empowers organizations to effortlessly create and manage utility expense budgets while forecasting future costs with precision. By leveraging historical data and trends, Joule ensures you have a clear, data-driven financial strategy to effectively control and plan for your utility expenses.

Energy management and tracking:

Joule not only tracks your energy usage but also expertly identifies opportunities for substantial savings. It helps you pinpoint the benefits of adopting energy-efficient equipment and optimizing usage patterns, ensuring you maximize energy efficiency and reduce costs effectively.

Cost allocation and chargeback:

Joule proactively keeps you in control by issuing alerts and notifications for critical events—whether it's when usage surpasses thresholds, bills are due, or any other significant occurrences. This ensures you stay ahead of potential issues and can take immediate action to manage your expenses effectively.

Bill management and payment:

Joule effortlessly integrates with building management systems and accounting software, delivering a cohesive and comprehensive view of your energy usage and costs. This integration ensures you have all the critical data you need in one place, enhancing your ability to optimize energy expenses effectively.

Alerts and notifications:

Joule streamlines cost management by enabling precise allocation of expenses by department, cost center, or building. It also simplifies the process of billing back costs to tenants or other departments, ensuring accurate financial tracking and clear accountability across your organization.

Compliance and reporting:

Joule ensures compliance with all relevant regulations and utility reporting requirements, enabling your organization meets legal standards effortlessly and remains up-to-date with mandatory reporting.
